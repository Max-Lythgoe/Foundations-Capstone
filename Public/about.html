PURPOSE

Maxed Out Music is a great new tool for finding and creating your unique music playlist! Find songs sorted by genre or other playlists on the home page, create your own playlist on the "My Playlist" page, or watch a short demo if you need any further assistance on the "About" page. We seek to help you find music you'll love and create a playlist so you'll have it all in one place. Also located in the top right are links to popular streaming platforms for an easy way to quickly change up your playlist on your respective streaming platform. Please enjoy the demo video below or read our "How To Use" section below that if you need any help navigating or using the site. Thanks for using Maxed Out Music!


HOW TO USE

Step 1

Click on one of our featured playlists on the "Home" page.


Step 2

Find a song that you would like to add to your playlist, and right click on the album art and select "Copy image address".


Step 3

Click the "My Playlist" button at the bottom of the playlist selection or at the navigation bar located at the top of the page.


Step 4

Enter in all song info including Title, Album Name, Artist, and lastly paste the image url that you copied in step 2.


Step 5

Finally, click the "Add Song" button. Voila! The song has been added to your playlist. If you would like to go back and find more songs click the "Back To Home" button at the bottom of the form and your playlist will remain saved as you check for new songs to add. You can always add more songs by simply searching google images for the album art and finding the image link that way. Once you are done creating your list you can click on the links at the top right of the page for your respective streaming service and create a playlist from there based off of the one you made on Maxed Out Music site.


Also, located in the bottom right of each page is a scroll-to-top button to save you some time!
